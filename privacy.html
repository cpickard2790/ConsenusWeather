Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Consensus Weather respects your privacy. This page explains what we (the app and its website) collect and how we use it.

What the app collects

  • Location (approx./precise, with your permission): Used to fetch local weather. Location is not stored on our servers. You can also search by city instead of sharing location.
  • Crash diagnostics: Apple may provide anonymized crash logs to help us fix issues.
  • Advertising (free version): We show contextual ads via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). We do not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and do not access the advertising identifier (IDFA). AdMob may process information such as IP address, device and app information, and network-derived approximate location to deliver and measure ads and may use Apple’s SKAdNetwork for attribution. See Google’s policy below.

What the app does not collect or do

  • No account is required, and we do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers inside the app.
  • No health, financial, or other sensitive personal data.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not sell personal data.

Third-party services

  • Weather data: Apple WeatherKit, Google Weather (Google Maps Platform), and U.S. National Weather Service (NWS).
  • Ads (free tier): Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Policy: Google Ads Policy.
  • Apple: App Store and crash diagnostics. Policy: Apple Privacy.

Data retention

We do not maintain a server-side user database. Any locally stored data (e.g., favorites, settings) stays on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Your choices

  • You can disable or change location permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Purchasing the Pro version removes ads.
